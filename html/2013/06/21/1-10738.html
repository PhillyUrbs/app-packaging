Note

1. Download the setup.exe from http://www.microsoft.com/typography/ClearTypePowerToy.mspx


2. Have filemon (http://www.sysinternals.com/Utilities/Filemon.html) monitor where setup.exe extracts the MSI-package when you execute it (or just search for "ClearType Tuning Control Panel Applet.msi" in your %TEMP%).


3. Copy the contents from the temp dir that setup.exe creates (includes the MSI + 3 ini-files).


4. Exit/Cancel the setup.exe installer.


5. Delete the _ISMSI*.INI, keep the other two ini-files.


6. Deploy. You don't need to change the MSI-package.
