Note

SecuraSoft NSF to PST converter is exclusively designed to transfer files from Lotus Notes to Outlook in bulk. With this NSF to PST tool, user can export complete data files of Emails, Contacts, Calendar, Task and To-do from Lotus Notes to Outlook without any changes in content & folder hierarchy. Take a look to the most exclusive properties of NSF to PST converter-:

  • Convert Multiple NSF files of different user id or Folders in a single run.
  • Proficiently remove encryption from Lotus Notes emails
  • Maintain the Meta Data as well as Folder Hierarchy of Lotus Notes mailboxes
  • Convert NSF to PST with Selective Email, Task, Contacts, and Calendar etc. with the help of Filter property.
  • Option to save names.nsf or address book of Lotus Notes in separate PST file.

What makes this NSF to PST tool  unique?

  • Transfer files from Lotus Notes to Outlook in Bulk
  • Auto Removal of Encryption from Lotus Notes Emails
  • Auto Split of NSF files if it is more than 20GB
  • Convert All Document Folder from Lotus Notes to Outlook
  • Exclude undesired data from the desired time period to make precise NSF to PST conversion
  • Demo version of NSF to PST converter offered to export 25 data items freely.
