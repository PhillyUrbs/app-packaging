Note
This is for the English version. .mst files may be needed for other languages.

REM CiscoIPCommunicator8.6.1
REM Office1 TFTP  x.y.z.12
REM Office2  TFTP x.y.z.15
REM Office3  TFTP x.y.z.17

msiexec /i  "%~dp0CiscoIPCommunicatorSetup.msi"   TFTP1="1x.y.z.12" TFTP2=" x.y.z.15"  /qn  REBOOT="ReallySuppress" /l*v "%windir%\logs\CiscoIPCommunicator8.6.1Install.log"




=====================
In my case, I used quotes areound IP addresses.
