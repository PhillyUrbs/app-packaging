    Note

    Change, apply or update the Asset Tag on your asset quickly and easily with BarKode.


    From the BarKode home screen select "Generic Search"


    Type in the serial number or asset tag of the asset you need to locate the asset record


    In the Asset record push the Asset Tag field to the left to display the scan button


    Scan your new Asset Tag


    Submit to Save the record and your new Asset Tag will be applied
