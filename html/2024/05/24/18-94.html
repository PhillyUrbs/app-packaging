    Installation silently for clients: SRsuite-Setup.exe /w /s /v /qn P_SETUPTYPE=Client /norestart

    Switches for Setup.exe


    The switch for Client Installation is not in default documentation. Also the switch /norestart.

    These came from Support.


    Installation silently for clients:

    SRsuite-Setup.exe /w /s /v /qn P_SETUPTYPE=Client /norestart
