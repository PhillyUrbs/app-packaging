    Note

    Aventail Connect can be deployed unattended by pre-setting your SonicWall connection settings in the ngsetup.ini file.

    Silent install command with customized IN (x64 version)I:  ngsetup64_en.exe -silent -f=ngsetup.ini

    (x86 version) ngsetup_en.exe -silent -f=ngsetup.ini
